PROJECT OVERVIEW.

Harmony Fest is a front-end web application dedicated to promoting and informing about Colombia's music festival, Harmony Fest, scheduled for the end of the year. The website serves as a platform to highlight the festival's essence, emphasizing its celebration of Colombia's diverse music genres. From the infectious beats of reggaeton with headlining artists like Karol G and Feid, to the passion and rhythm of salsa, carranga, and merengue, Harmony Fest promises an exhilarating experience for music enthusiasts.

The website comprises several sections:

  1. Introduction: Provides an overview of Harmony Fest, explaining why it's a must-attend event, especially for those who appreciate Colombia's rich musical heritage.
  2. Lineup: Displays the schedule and timings of the artists' performances, allowing visitors to plan their festival experience accordingly.
  3. Gallery: Features a visually appealing gallery section with pop-up windows showcasing captivating images from past festivals, offering a glimpse into the vibrant atmosphere.
  4. Tickets: Presents details of VIP and general admission tickets, including inclusions and pricing, allowing users to make informed decisions about ticket purchases.
