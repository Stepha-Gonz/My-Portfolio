PROJECT OVERVIEW

My portfolio is a front-end project designed to showcase my skills in programming and web design. The site includes detailed sections on my education, experience, and the various tools and technologies I use, such as HTML, JavaScript, CSS, SASS, Gulp, and Webpack. It also features an array of projects, ranging from front-end to full-stack and data analysis.

Each project in the portfolio is accompanied by a description, relevant links to their GitHub repositories, and live demos where available. For projects without live links, video demonstrations are provided to give a comprehensive overview of their functionality and design. This ensures that visitors can thoroughly understand the scope and quality of my work.

The portfolio is designed with performance in mind, utilizing tools like Gulp and Webpack for optimization. The layout is user-friendly and visually appealing, making it easy for potential employers and collaborators to navigate and explore my work. By showcasing a variety of projects and skill sets, my portfolio demonstrates my versatility and proficiency in both front-end and back-end development, as well as data analysis. It serves as a comprehensive representation of my abilities and professional journey in the field of web development.
