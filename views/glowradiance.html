PROJECT OVERVIEW

Glowradiance s a full-stack solution for beauty salons, employing a Model-View-Controller (MVC) architecture. It offers a seamless experience for clients and administrators, streamlining appointment scheduling and providing robust tools for service management and appointment tracking.

Administrators can easily add or remove services, oversee appointments, and maintain a comprehensive catalog. Notably, Glowradiance prioritizes security with user authentication, password validation, and token-based authentication via email. Its intuitive interface and well-engineered architecture ensure scalability and ease of maintenance.

In essence, Glowradiance is a commitment to enhancing salon operations and customer experience with comprehensive features and elegant design.
