BizExpo

DESCRIPTION.
PROJECT OVERVIEW.

BizExpo is a dynamic web platform designed for managing a technology event featuring conferences and workshops. The site provides a comprehensive overview of the event, including detailed schedules, speaker profiles, and venue information displayed on an interactive Leaflet map. Users can register and verify their accounts via email, then access a range of ticket options, including virtual, in-person, and free passes. Payment for virtual or paid tickets is handled through PayPal, and users are directed to their tickets or event choices based on their selection.

For administrators, BizExpo offers a robust admin panel with data analytics, including recent registrations, revenue from PayPal, and availability of event seats. The admin panel also features CRUD operations for managing speakers, users, and events, with pagination for ease of navigation. Additionally, there is a section for tracking gift distribution among users, presented through graphical insights.
